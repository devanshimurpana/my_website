  • What is it?

    Organize and succeed is a start-up company that makes organizers and planners to help children, students, and adults to manage their time more efficiently.

    Planners are customized to suit the customer's schedule and help them optimize their productivity.

  • Why I created it

    • I created this business as a way to express my passion in organizing and to help other people realise the benefits of creating a flexible system in addition to efficiently completitng tasks.

  • How I created it

    I made use of Canva, Microsoft Office and Adobe to design my planners.

  • When

    O&S was active from March 2019- September 2020.

  • Target audience

    My target audience started off with high-school students with daily task planners. Evenetually I designed work and meal planners for adults and even colorful planenrs for kids.
